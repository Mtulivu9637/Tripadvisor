About Us

Who We Are

TripAdvisor is the world's largest travel site, enabling travelers to plan and book the perfect trip. TripAdvisor offers advice from millions of travelers and a wide variety of travel choices and planning features with seamless links to booking tools that check hundreds of websites to find the best hotel prices. TripAdvisor branded sites make up the largest travel community in the world, reaching 490 million average monthly unique visitors, and reached 795 million reviews and opinions covering 8.4 million accommodations, restaurants and attractions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help people around the world plan and have the perfect trip.
